Ema datse

Ema datshi (Dzongkha:ཨེ་མ་དར་ཚིལ་; Wylie: e-ma dar-tshil[1]) is a Bhutanese dish made from hot chili peppers and cheese. It is among the most famous dishes in Bhutanese cuisine, recognized as a national dish of Bhutan.[2] "Ema" means "chili" and "datshi" means "cheese"[3] in the Dzongkha language of Bhutan.
